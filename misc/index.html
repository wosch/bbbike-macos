Get the full power of BBBike and download the standalone perl/tk version!
  • For MacOS 10.5.X/Intel, MacOS 10.5.X/PowerPC: to start BBBike open the image and click on the city name of your choise.
  • Other operating systems: download binaries, data for other cities. 
      Extract data: tar xfz data-osm.tgz
  Start bbbike with your city of choise: bbbike -datadir data-osm/Copenhagen

Known Problems

!!!Keep in mind this is a real BETA service!!! You may be the second person who use it.
  • Character set: utf8, latin2 not fully supported
  • Translation: some messages are only in German language
  • Routing: some streets are not connected at bridges
  • Berlin: many cool features are only available for Berlin
  • Development: this is work in progress. The site may crash.
  • Speed: The CGI script bbbike.cgi is not optimized for speed and is a little bit slow for large cities
