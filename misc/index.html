BBBike @ World - A Route Planner for Cyclists

BBBike is a route planner for cyclists in Berlin. It is now ported to other cities around the world - thanks to the OpenStreetMap project!

Orginal BBBike data: Berlin

Get the full power of BBBike and download the standalone perl/tk version!
  • For MacOS 10.5/Intel, MacOS 10.5/PowerPC:
    open the image to start BBBike and click on the city name of your choise.

  • Other operating systems (FreeBSD, Debian, Windows): download binaries and data for other cities. 

      Extract data: tar xfz data-osm.tgz
  Start bbbike with your city of choise: bbbike -datadir data-osm/Copenhagen

Known Problems

!!!Keep in mind this is a real BETA service!!! You may be the second person who use it.
  • Character set: utf8, latin2 not fully supported
  • Routing: some streets are not connected at bridges
  • Berlin: many cool features are only available for Berlin
